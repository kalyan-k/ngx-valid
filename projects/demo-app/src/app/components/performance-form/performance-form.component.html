Performance Form — Dynamic Validation Benchmark

How this demo works
  • Configure the number of sections (1–50) and controls per section (1–200), then click Generate form.
  • Incremental render — sections and controls are mounted in batches so large forms (e.g. 2,500 controls) stay responsive.
  • Grouped validation — each section is its own policy and form group, aggregated by the performance policy group.
  • Benchmarks — generation, render, validate-all, and submit timings are captured below.
